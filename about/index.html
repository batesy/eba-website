About Us

EBA was founded in 2016 by Michael Poulos and Patrick Lowe. Michael and Patrick are international businessmen from Canada with over 20 cumulative years of teaching experience. Understanding and appreciating the value of English in the workplace, their objective was to offer programs and courses focused on a superior educational experience for business professionals in Spain. As a result, Michael and Patrick founded EBA, with the common goal of preparing professionals to work in an English environment.

About Our Teachers

At EBA we pride ourselves on our selection process to ensure that we are providing you with the best quality teacher for your needs. We provide teachers of different ages, nationalities and accents based on your preferences that are all focused on taking your English to the next level.
