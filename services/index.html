Our Services

English Business Academy is an educational institution that specializes in providing you and your business with the skills needed to successfully communicate and connect in English. With the ongoing globalization of our world, it has never been more important to learn the world’s most international language.

At EBA, through a variety of courses and services, we will prepare you and your company to successfully communicate in English. EBA takes a personalized approach with our classes to ensure that you are getting a service that best suits you, your level and your needs.

We offer one-on- one courses as well as group courses. Furthermore, at EBA, we come to you! There is no need to waste time commuting to class because our teachers go to your business on your schedule.
